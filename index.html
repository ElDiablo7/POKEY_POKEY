      Street Dice Rules

      Street Rules (full game)

      Two dice. You bet, then roll.

      • Come-out roll: 7 or 11 = win instantly. 2, 3, or 12 = lose (craps).
      • Point: 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, or 10 becomes your "point."
      • Point phase: Keep rolling until you hit your point (win) or roll 7 (lose).

      Prison Rules (single roll)

      Simpler: one roll decides all.

      • 7 or 11 = Win
      • 2, 3, or 12 = Lose (craps)
      • 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, or 10 = Push (tie, bet returned)
